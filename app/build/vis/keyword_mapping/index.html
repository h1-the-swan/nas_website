Keyword Mapping

About the Keyword Mapping

The map shows the co-occurrence of keywords extracted from the titles and abstracts of the seed set. The map only shows keywords that appear in at least 5 papers. This is a static visualization (non-interactive).


This online, interactive report was created by Jevin West, Jason Portenoy, and the National Academy of Sciences. Please contact us if you have any questions or comments about the tools or other content.
